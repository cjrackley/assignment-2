Top 3 Restaurant to visit at UNCG

Welcome to the UNCG Campus Food Blog

There are lots of good places around Greensboro to get a bite. As a college student, it can be hard to find places that are affordable and still tasty! Below are 3 restaurants that might peak your interest without breaking the bank. Be sure to visit at least one of these!

EUC Chick-fil-a

Location: 516 Stirling St
Hours: 7:30AM-8PM

You can't ever go wrong with Chick-fil-a. Open daily during the school year, this place can be the perfect place to grab some food. It has lots of seating and is located in the EUC building right on campus!

Chick-fil-a Chicken Sandwich

Chicken patty on a buttered bun.

Mac & Cheese

Creamy chedder-style mac & cheese.

Hand-Spun Milkshake

Flavors include: Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, and Cookies & Cream

Manhattan Pizza & Subs

Location: 449 Tate St
Hours: 12PM-3AM

Located just off campus, Manhattan Subs is a quick walk for some decent food. The hours are unmatched, being the only restaurant in the area that is open as late as it is.

Cheese Pizza

Classic 10" pizza with a doughy crust

Philly Steak & Cheese Sub

Chopped steak matched with provolone cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, mayo & hourse dressing.

Wings & Waffle Fries Special

Choice of 5 or 10 wings with a side of waffle fries.

Cici's Pizza

Location: 3379 Battleground Ave
Hours: 11AM-10PM

Although this list already has a restaurant with pizza, I had to add this one. Located off one of the major roads in Greensboro, its a quick drive for all you can eat pizza!

Create your own pizza

Offering a wide variety of toppings

Baked pasta

Pasta with a choice of Marinara or Alfredo Sauce

Fudge Brownies

Rich, fudgy brownies topped with powdered sugar

Extra Food Tips

  • Some restaurants on Tate Street accept Flex and offer student discounts
  • If late night runs to Manhattan Subs doesn't appeal to you, UNCG offers a 24 hour self-serve food and drink station
  • Restaurant's aren't for everyone, so for those who prefer to make food at home, UNCG has their very own general-store!
